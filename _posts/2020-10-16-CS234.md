    - Markov Processes  <br>
        S is a (finite) set of states <br> 
        P is dynamics/transition models that specifieces p(st+1 = s' | st = s)
        No rewards, No actions 
    - Markov Reward Processes (MRPs) <br>
        S , P

        Discont factor r in [0,1]
